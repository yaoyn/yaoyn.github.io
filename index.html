Biography

Yinuo Yao (Noah) is a postdoctoral researcher, advised by Prof. Ilenia Battiato in Energy Resources Engineering at Stanford University. His research interests include environmental biotechnology, computational fluid dynamics, mathematical upscaling theory, artificial intelligence and water and wastewater treatment.

Before coming to the United States, I spent my childhood in a small and cozy town in China and my teenage years and college in Singapore’s tropical island country. At Stanford, I am co-advised by Prof. Craig Criddle and Prof. Oliver Fringer conducted research at the interface of fluid dynamics and environmental biotechnology to address the sustainability issues in the 21st century. Outside research and the lab, I enjoy playing tennis and hiking and have also been reading mangas since my teenage years.
